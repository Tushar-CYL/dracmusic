How to Create a Playlist

To create a playlist, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. Go to the "Playlist" section.
  3. Click the "Create New Playlist" button.
  4. Give your playlist a name and description.
  5. Search for songs and add them to your playlist.
  6. Click "Save" to save your playlist.

How to Find New Music

Discover new music by:

  1. Browsing through the "New Releases" section.
  2. Exploring the "Recommended for You" section based on your listening history.
  3. Using the search bar to find artists, albums, or tracks.
